MusicWoob ("we", "us", "our") operates https://musicwoob.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage it. By using the Site and our services (together, the "Services"), you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account info: name, email, password, profile image and other details you submit when creating an account.
  • Content you upload: audio files, artwork, track metadata, tags and other materials you post.
  • Communications: support requests, feedback, contest entries and other messages.
  • Payment details: payment or billing information provided when making purchases (we use third-party processors and do not store full card numbers).

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, features used, session duration, play counts and related telemetry.
  • Device & connection data: IP address, browser, device type, OS, language, time zone and approximate location.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: to enable site functionality, analytics and preferences.

1.3 Third-party sources

We may receive information from third-party services you connect with (social login providers), analytics vendors, advertising partners, and payment processors.

2. How we use your information

We use data to:

  • Provide, operate and improve the Services.
  • Create and manage your account and provide customer support.
  • Process transactions and send confirmations or receipts.
  • Personalize content, recommendations and search results.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal bases for processing (EEA/GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, we rely on these legal bases: performance of a contract, consent (where requested), our legitimate interests (balanced against your rights), and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Sharing and disclosure

We may share information with:

  • Service providers: hosting, payment processors, analytics, customer support and other vendors that assist us.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, sale, or similar corporate transaction.
  • Legal requests: to comply with laws or enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Public content: anything you post publicly may be visible to others.

We do not sell personal information for money. If our practices change, we will update this policy and provide notice as required by law.

5. Third-party services and links

Our Site may link to third-party websites and services. Those external sites have their own privacy practices; we encourage you to review their policies before sharing personal information.

6. Cookies & tracking technologies

We and our partners use cookies, web beacons and similar technologies to enable functionality, remember preferences, analyze usage and deliver targeted advertising where permitted. You can control cookies through your browser settings and any consent controls we provide. Disabling some cookies may affect Site features.

7. Data security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect personal data. However, no system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs that affects your rights or freedoms, we will notify affected individuals and regulators as required by law.

8. Data retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements. When no longer needed, data will be deleted or anonymized.

9. Children’s privacy

Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us so we can remove it.

Access, correction, deletion

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us and we will verify your identity before responding.

Marketing preferences

You can opt out of promotional emails via the unsubscribe link in emails or by updating your account settings. Transactional emails (account, billing, safety) cannot be opted out of.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under state law including the right to know, delete and opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for targeted advertising. Submit requests to our contact below; we will verify identity as required.

12. International transfers

MusicWoob is based in India and may transfer data to jurisdictions with differing laws. Where required, we implement safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) to protect transfers.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with a new effective date and, where appropriate, additional notice.
