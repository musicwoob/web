SHITIZ

(Founder & CEO - MusicWoob)

Shitiz was born on July 18, 2005, in Amlai, a small town in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. Raised in a nurturing environment, Shitiz pursued his education with dedication and ambition. He completed his 10th-grade education from Sambhawna Residential High School, affiliated with the CBSE board, and later moved to Bihar board, where he completed his 12th-grade education from Ram Nagina Pandey +2 High School. Driven by a passion for music and business, Shitiz took a bold step in 2024 by founding MusicWoob Private Limited, a music company where he serves as the Founder and CEO. His vision for the company is ambitious: to advance the music industry to new heights and to create the world's largest music group. As a producer and businessman, Shitiz combines his love for music with sharp business acumen, making him a rising star in the industry. In addition to his role at MusicWoob, Shitiz is a business coach who mentors new startups and helps talented artists reach a larger audience, enabling them to gain the recognition they deserve. His efforts in guiding emerging talent have made a significant impact in the music industry. Shitiz's interests are diverse. While he has a little interest in politics, he is deeply invested in Bollywood, the film industry, and the music industry. His love for these fields fuels his ambitions and inspires him to innovate. In pursuit of knowledge and skills, Shitiz has completed numerous courses from top educational institutes. His qualifications include digital marketing, web and app development, ethical hacking, marketing, cloud management, personality development, and more. These skills have equipped him with the tools necessary to succeed in the fast-paced and ever-evolving business world. Shitiz's family has been a strong support system in his journey. His father is an advocate in the Civil Court of Ara City, and his mother is a homemaker. Together, they have nurtured Shitiz's talents and ambitions, helping him to become the accomplished individual he is today. With a bright future ahead, Shitiz continues to strive for excellence, driven by his dreams of revolutionizing the music industry and making a lasting impact on the world.
